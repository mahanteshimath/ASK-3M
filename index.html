Workflow

How Ask 3M Works

A step-by-step journey from design challenge to optimal material solution.

1

Ask Your Design Challenge

Engineers describe their bonding or assembly challenge in natural language. The AI understands context about substrates, environmental conditions, load requirements, and design constraints ‚Äî just like talking to an expert.

2

Ask 3M Reviews Your Use Case

The AI assistant analyzes the engineer's specific requirements ‚Äî questioning substrates, environments, assembly methods, and performance targets. It leverages 3M's decades of material science knowledge to understand the problem deeply.

3

AI Recommends Optimal Solutions

Based on the analysis, Ask 3M recommends the most suitable adhesives, tapes, or materials from 3M's portfolio. Each recommendation is backed by technical data and designed to meet the specific performance criteria.

4

Digital Validation & Simulation

Engineers can digitally validate recommended materials using simulation-ready data cards before investing in physical prototypes. Integration with the 3M Digital Materials Hub enables virtual testing and optical model simulation.

5

Prototype & Purchase

Small quantity purchase options allow for final, confident prototyping. Engineers can quickly move from digital selection to physical testing with accelerated timeframes.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Ask 3M available to the public? ‚ñº
Ask 3M is currently in pilot phase, primarily with engineers within 3M's Safety & Industrial Business Group. 3M is accepting sign-ups on their official website to be notified when broader access becomes available. The tool debuted at CES 2026 in January.
What types of materials does Ask 3M cover? ‚ñº
Initially, Ask 3M focuses on adhesives and tapes for bonding design challenges. This includes structural adhesives, VHB tapes, double-coated tapes, adhesive transfer tapes, and various specialty bonding solutions. 3M has stated that additional applications, industries, and workflows are being added over time.
How does Ask 3M differ from a regular chatbot? ‚ñº
Unlike generic chatbots, Ask 3M is purpose-built for engineering workflows. It has deep knowledge of 3M's material science portfolio, understands technical parameters (substrates, environmental conditions, load requirements), and provides simulation-ready data. It's more like having an expert 3M technical engineer available 24/7 than a customer service bot.
What cloud technology powers Ask 3M? ‚ñº
Ask 3M is built on Amazon Web Services (AWS) AI capabilities, specifically Amazon Bedrock for foundation model access and Amazon Bedrock AgentCore for enterprise-grade agent deployment, security, memory management, and serverless execution.
Can Ask 3M create materials that don't exist? ‚ñº
Through the expanded 3M Digital Materials Hub, customers can request bespoke virtual materials ‚Äî technologies that solve specific design challenges but don't yet exist. 3M leverages its decades of material science expertise to accelerate the development and delivery of these unique solutions.
What is the 3M Digital Materials Hub? ‚ñº
The Digital Materials Hub is a companion platform to Ask 3M. Launched in early 2025 and expanded for CES 2026, it provides access to verified material data cards, mechanical models, optical models, and virtual materials. It also features a Workbench for direct collaboration with 3M scientists and has 300+ AI-powered product model combinations.
